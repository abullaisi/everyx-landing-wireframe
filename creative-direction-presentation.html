The Purpose
of This Step

As with any part of the creative process, alignment is key. This step is designed to paint a picture of what EveryX's visual identity could look like, through the curation of references and inspiration. This is for us to collaborate on what approach might suit the vision best, and work to attract the right audience. It is the first step in bringing the brand to life.

Introduction 002 Creative Direction

About
the Brand

EveryX is the world's first prediction market with leverage (up to 10x). Built on dual-chain (BNB + Polygon), self-funded, Philippines-first. At its core: leveraged event trading, AI-driven probability baselines, and esports-rooted competitive culture. The ambition is bigger than a trading platform - it is sentiment data infrastructure.

Clarity

Probability-first UI. Three simple choices instead of order books.

Fairness

Pool-based pricing. Trade the crowd, not a market maker.

Capital Efficiency

Leverage with explicit max loss. No interest on leveraged positions.

Collective Intelligence

Financially backed forecasting that generates crowd-priced data as a public good.

About The Brand 003 Creative Direction

Our Goal

EveryX owns a new rung on the category ladder: Leveraged Prediction Markets. The visual identity must signal that difference without announcing it. The brand should read as a serious tool first, an exciting platform second. The design goal is precision over decoration, infrastructure over hype, and competitive culture held beneath a clean surface.

Overview & Goals 005 Creative Direction

Concept 01
Signal

Signal - Precision - Infrastructure

EveryX is the forecasting tool for traders who have done the research. Every design decision reads infrastructure first - data precise, minimal, authoritative. The volt green is deployed sparingly as the signal color: it marks what matters.
Colour
#0A0A0A Near black #C41230 Brand red (cover + hero) #94F201 Volt green (UI signal only) #F0F0F0 Off-white text
Type
Display and wordmark: Space Grotesk Bold. UI and body: Geist. Data and live figures: Geist Mono.
Imagery
Data visualization, probability curves, dark studio photography.
Texture
No organic texture. Fine grid or dot-matrix as structural background element only.

Overview & Goals 007 Creative Direction

Concept 02
Arena

Arena - Conviction - PVP Culture

EveryX as competitive arena. The brand leans into the PVP trading identity rooted in esports culture - bold, fast, high-contrast. Green is more aggressive here, used as energy rather than signal.
Colour
#0A0A0A Near black #C41230 Brand red (energy fills) #94F201 Volt green (live indicators) #1A1A1A Card surface
Type
Display and wordmark: DM Sans ExtraBold. UI and body: DM Sans Regular. Hero numbers and leaderboard figures: Bebas Neue (display use only).
Imagery
Esports event photography, competitive screens, leaderboard UI, high-action moments.
Texture
Scan lines or fine grid at low opacity. Subtle noise overlay on dark surfaces.
